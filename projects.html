Refreshing a 90 year brand

The Brotherhood of St. Laurence (BSL) is a social justice organisation working to prevent and alleviate poverty across Australia. The problem was that the purpose of the BSL Website was not clear and many did not favour the aesthetic choice, design and layout of the homepage.

View Website View Mobile

Lets talk design

Insights

Our reach found that the what was important for both users and BSL is to deliver their core message clearly and connect it to their services by using colour and icons and 1:1 focused services.

Design iterations

Through each design iteration, we were able to consolidate they key features we needed to address the pain points in our user research. Playing with layout, colour and navigation was key in finding the most ideal design for BSL and people.

Style

Keeping the style for the 90 years was essential for keeping the familiarity and consistency through out. However we remixed the hero colours to give the website true refresh.

Final Output

We presented the changes to BSL and they were delighted at the changes and have taken it under consideration under the new construction for their future website. Will keep you posted if we have any updates about construction.
